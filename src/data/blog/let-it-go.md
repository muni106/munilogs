# Let things go

I'm recently experiencing a moment of change, I'm currently changing a lot, my mindset, my body, my skill level.
I found out that the worst part is that you have to abandon things which were part of the past every time you want to make a big change.
I'm not suggesting that changing is always good, but when you want to push yourself is the only effective option, in fact my life has always been a story of how bad I felt in enjoying the comfort, and I think that is also my biggest strength.
